      HT Meter Panels

      Akshita Associates designs and manufactures precision-engineered HT Meter Panels for accurate energy measurement in high-voltage applications up to 33kV. Our panels feature class 0.2S/0.5S accuracy meters with potential and current transformers, enclosed in robust CRCA/GI cabinets with IP54 protection for reliable operation in harsh environments. These comprehensive metering solutions provide detailed monitoring of active/reactive energy, maximum demand, power factor and other critical parameters, complete with tamper-proof features and event logging capabilities. Compliant with IEC 62053 and IS 13779 standards, our HT Meter Panels are ideal for substations, independent power producers and large industrial consumers, offering both local display and remote SCADA connectivity options for seamless integration into modern energy management systems. With customizable configurations for outdoor/indoor installation and optional surge protection devices, we deliver turnkey metering solutions that ensure billing accuracy and power quality analysis for high-voltage networks.
